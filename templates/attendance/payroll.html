How Payroll is Calculated:
  • Generates draft slips for all active users (except Owner).
  • Scans calendar days, marking absents / LOP leave days as unpaid.
  • Deductions = (Absent days * LOP rate) + (Late days * Late rate).
  • Adjusted against Base Salary to produce Net Payable Salary.
